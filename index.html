About Orange Jordan

Orange Jordan is one of Orange Group’s subsidiaries in 26 countries, one of the world’s top ten operators, with 137,000 employees serving a total customer base of 298 million customers worldwide (as on 31 December 2023) and sales of 44.1 billion euros at the end of 2023. Orange offers a wide range of information technology and telecommunication services not only to the individuals, but also to businesses under the brand Orange Business. As the digital partner of the Kingdom, Orange continues to drive digital transformation by introducing the most advanced technologies, robust infrastructure and innovative solutions, available through its 61 shops and 38 smart selling points across the kingdom, as well as its various digital channels.
